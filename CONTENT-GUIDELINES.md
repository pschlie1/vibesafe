# Scantient Content Guidelines

## Writing Style

- Use clear, simple language.
- Be spartan and informative.
- Use short, impactful sentences.
- Use active voice. Avoid passive voice.
- Focus on practical, actionable insights.
- Use bullet point lists in social media posts.
- Use data and examples to support claims when possible.
- Use "you" and "your" to directly address the reader.

## Punctuation

- Never use em dashes. Use commas, periods, or semicolons to connect ideas.
- No semicolons.

## Avoid These Constructions

- "...not just this, but also this"
- Metaphors and cliches
- Generalizations
- Common setup language: "in conclusion", "in closing", etc.
- Unnecessary adjectives and adverbs
- Hashtags
- Markdown formatting
- Asterisks
