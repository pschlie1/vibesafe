## Target List (research starting points)

**LinkedIn search queries:**
- "IT Director" + "Cursor" (people who mention Cursor in their profile/posts)
- "CISO" + "AI development" + company size 100-1,000
- "VP Engineering" + "vibe coding" OR "AI apps" + regulated industry

**Where to find them:**
- The HN Cursor/compliance thread — people commenting are your exact ICP
- Reddit r/sysadmin, r/msp — IT managers venting about shadow IT
- LinkedIn posts about "my developers are shipping AI apps" — these people have budget and pain

**Qualification criteria:**
- Company uses AI coding tools (Cursor, Lovable, Replit, GitHub Copilot)
- Has compliance requirements (SOC2, HIPAA, ISO, PCI)
- Has > 10 developers
- IT leader has budget authority (Director level or above)
- NOT a pure security company (they'll want deep pentesting, not governance)
